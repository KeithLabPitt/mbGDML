compute_force_similarity(predict_force, true_force)

Compute modified cosine distance of two force vectors.

Computes 1 - cosine_similarity. Two exact vectors will thus have a similarity of 0, orthogal vectors will be 1, and equal but opposite will be 2.

Parameters:
  • predict_force (np.ndarray) – Array of the predicted force vector by GDML.
  • true_force (np.ndarray) – Array of the true force vector of the same shape as predict_force
Returns:

Similarity (accuracy) of predicted force vector for an atom. 0.0 is perfect similarity and the further away from zero the less similar (accurate).
Return type:

float
